This is the Middle College Essay Resources Website! Created and maintained by past, current, and future students of the Middle College program at West Valley College! We thank you for your use and support of this website!

If you are interested in helping with development and maintenance, please consider joining the Discord, or contacting the contribution leaders directly. If not, then continue scrolling for more information on what this site has to offer!

How to Use

The website allows you to submit an essay in the "Essay" page, or submit your document for one-time use when using the "Tools," the Essay section contains various links to the other addons and tools to format your essay, as well as real-time checking of all possible format errors (not grammar errors) within your document. In some ways, it serves as the "hub" for what this website provides. the tools, on the other hand, serve as grab-and-go solutions to many tedious parts of essay writing.
